SimonSays

Simon says is a game where one of the four lights are selected in a sequence. Player must follow sequence just like Simon to go to next round.

Coded by

Fernanda Correa at General Assembly WDI 2015. Game done with javascript, jquery, html5, and css3.

To play

Press start to play. You have 30 second to complete each round.
